Peacock

ONLY THE MALES ARE ACTUALLY “PEACOCKS.”

    Scientific Name: Pavo
    Egg Incubation: 28-30 days
    Average Lifespan: 10-25 years
    Length: 2.0 to 9.8 ft

The Peacock, also known as the peafowl, can grow their "tail" or "train" feathers up to six feet in length. That makes up about 60% of its body length! Despite these odd proportions, their abllity for flight is minimal.
